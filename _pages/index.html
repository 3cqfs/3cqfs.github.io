The Tricontinental Quantum Fundamentals Seminar (3C-QFS) series is a fortnightly meeting online, which aims to connect researchers from Australia, Europe and America.

Our goal is to facilitate discussion between the three continents and bring together those working on foundational topics in relativistic quantum physics, covering but not limited to:

  • Measurement, localisation, and causality in relativistic quantum theory
  • Energy conditions and quantum inequalities
  • Non-locality and observer-dependence of entanglement
  • Quantum reference frames and symmetries in quantum theory
  • Holography, AdS/CFT, and analogue gravity

For 3C-QFS, we wish to foster an inclusive community and promote open discussion by encouraging a casual atmosphere during the seminars.

Upcoming Seminars

The seminars will be held online via Zoom, consisting of a 40-60 minute talk followed by additional time for questions.
